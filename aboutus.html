Bharata Mata College of Commerce and Arts

Bharata Mata College of Commerce and Arts, Choondy, Aluva, established in 2014, is a private self-financing degree college of commerce and arts in the unaided sector, owned and managed by the Archdiocese of Ernakulam-Angamaly. Located near Choondy Junction, Aluva, it is easily accessible from Aluva, Perumbavoor, and Ernakulam.

The college offers degree courses in English, Computer Application, and Business Administration along with Commerce, with specializations in Taxation, Marketing, and Computer Application.The college is affiliated with Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam, and is recognized by the Govt. of Kerala.

Our mission is to equip the young generation with personal and professional excellence as well as social commitment to meet global challenges through continuous teaching-learning-training programs and value-based activities, ensuring satisfaction for all. BMCCA's core values include respect for all, integrity and honesty, innovation and creativity, and a passion for achieving new heights.

Our quality policy is to achieve and sustain exemplary standards in education by improving student success through a structured coaching system, competitive and committed teachers, effective teaching methodologies. BMCCA upholds high Christian values.
